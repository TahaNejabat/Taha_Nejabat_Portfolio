// About

MULTI-
DISC-
IPLINARY

I'm a designer whose work spans automotive form, transport futures, and human-centred UX — built on an MA from Coventry University and a foundation in visual communication and architecture.

My Blender expertise runs deep enough that I build my own tools — custom add-ons that improve workflow speed, precision, and control. I combine that with Gravity Sketch, Vizcom, Alias, and AI-assisted pipelines to push ideas further, faster.

Research is as important to me as craft. Whether analysing future mobility scenarios, exploring cultural patterns, or running user interviews — I see research as the foundation of every creative decision.

I'm Iranian, based in Coventry UK, with a brother in Sweden and strong ties to Scandinavian design culture. I'm actively pursuing design roles in Sweden and internationally.

Outside work: Interstellar, Tolkien, Linkin Park, Uncharted, and instrumental music as ScriptThorix.
